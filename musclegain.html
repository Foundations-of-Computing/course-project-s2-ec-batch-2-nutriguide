Muscle Gain Nutrition Guide

Tips for Building Muscle

  • Consume a high-protein diet with lean meats, dairy, and plant-based proteins.
  • Increase healthy carbs like quinoa, brown rice, and whole wheat bread.
  • Lift heavy weights and follow a structured workout plan.
  • Drink protein shakes and high-calorie smoothies for extra muscle fuel.
  • Get enough sleep and rest for proper muscle recovery.

High-Protein Drinks for Muscle Gain

1. Chocolate Protein Shake

Ingredients: Protein powder, banana, cocoa powder, milk, peanut butter.

Instructions: Blend all ingredients until smooth and creamy.

2. Almond Butter & Oats Shake

Ingredients: Almond butter, oats, milk, honey, protein powder.

Instructions: Blend and serve chilled.

3. Greek Yogurt & Berries Smoothie

Ingredients: Greek yogurt, mixed berries, honey, chia seeds.

Instructions: Blend all ingredients and enjoy.

High-Protein Meals for Muscle Growth

1. Grilled Chicken & Brown Rice

Ingredients: Chicken breast, brown rice, steamed broccoli, olive oil.

Instructions: Grill chicken, cook brown rice, and serve with broccoli.

2. Lentil & Quinoa Power Bowl

Ingredients: Lentils, quinoa, roasted veggies, nuts.

Instructions: Cook lentils and quinoa, mix with roasted veggies.

3. Scrambled Eggs & Whole Wheat Toast

Ingredients: Eggs, whole wheat bread, avocado, butter.

Instructions: Scramble eggs, serve with avocado toast.

4. Paneer & Spinach Stir-Fry

Ingredients: Paneer cubes, spinach, garlic, olive oil.

Instructions: Stir-fry paneer and spinach with garlic.

Protein-Packed Salads for Muscle Gain

1. Chickpea & Avocado Salad

Ingredients: Chickpeas, avocado, olive oil, lemon.

Instructions: Mix all ingredients and enjoy.

2. Tuna & Quinoa Salad

Ingredients: Quinoa, tuna, cherry tomatoes, olive oil.

Instructions: Toss everything together and serve.

3. Egg & Spinach Salad

Ingredients: Boiled eggs, spinach, feta cheese, olive oil.

Instructions: Mix all ingredients and drizzle with olive oil.
