Healthy Weight Gain Guide

Tips for Gaining Healthy Weight

  • Eat calorie-dense foods with good fats, proteins, and carbs.
  • Increase portion sizes and eat more frequently.
  • Consume healthy snacks like nuts, cheese, and protein bars.
  • Include strength training exercises to build muscle mass.
  • Drink smoothies and shakes instead of plain water.

High-Calorie Drinks for Weight Gain

1. Peanut Butter Banana Shake

Ingredients: Banana, peanut butter, milk, oats, honey.

Instructions: Blend all ingredients until smooth and creamy.

2. Chocolate Avocado Smoothie

Ingredients: Avocado, cocoa powder, milk, honey.

Instructions: Blend ingredients until smooth and enjoy.

3. Almond Date Protein Shake

Ingredients: Almonds, dates, protein powder, milk.

Instructions: Blend all ingredients and serve chilled.

Healthy Meals for Weight Gain

1. Avocado & Egg Toast

Ingredients: Whole wheat bread, avocado, boiled eggs, olive oil.

Instructions: Mash avocado on toast, top with eggs, and drizzle with olive oil.

2. Quinoa & Lentil Bowl

Ingredients: Quinoa, lentils, roasted vegetables, olive oil.

Instructions: Cook quinoa and lentils, mix with roasted veggies.

3. Greek Yogurt & Granola Bowl

Ingredients: Greek yogurt, granola, honey, nuts.

Instructions: Mix Greek yogurt with granola and top with honey & nuts.

4. Paneer & Sweet Potato Stir-Fry

Ingredients: Paneer, sweet potatoes, bell peppers, olive oil.

Instructions: Stir-fry paneer and vegetables in olive oil.

Healthy Salads for Weight Gain

1. Avocado & Chickpea Salad

Ingredients: Avocado, chickpeas, olive oil, lemon juice.

Instructions: Mix ingredients and enjoy.

2. Cheese & Walnut Salad

Ingredients: Cheese cubes, walnuts, spinach, olive oil.

Instructions: Toss everything together and serve.

3. Pasta & Chicken Salad

Ingredients: Whole wheat pasta, grilled chicken, olive oil, veggies.

Instructions: Cook pasta, mix with chicken and veggies.
